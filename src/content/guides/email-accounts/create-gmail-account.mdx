## Before You Start

Gmail is Google's free email service. Creating an account also gives you access to YouTube, Google Drive, Google Photos, and the Google Play Store — all with one login.

**What you'll need:**
- A phone or computer with internet access
- A phone number (for verification — you only need this once)
- A name you want to use for your email address

## Step 1: Go to the Gmail Sign-Up Page

Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox) and go to:

**gmail.com**

On the page that loads, click the blue **Create account** button. Choose **For my personal use** from the dropdown.

## Step 2: Enter Your Name

1. Type your **First name** and **Last name** in the boxes
2. Click **Next**

This name will appear on emails you send. You can use your real name or a nickname — whatever you want recipients to see.

## Step 3: Choose Your Email Address

1. Type the email address you want — for example, `yourname@gmail.com`
2. If that address is already taken, Google will suggest alternatives with numbers added (like `yourname123@gmail.com`)
3. You can also click **Create your own Gmail address** to try different variations

> **Tip:** Pick something professional if you plan to use this for job applications or business. `firstname.lastname@gmail.com` is a good format if it's available.

## Step 4: Create a Password

1. Type a password that's at least 8 characters
2. Type it again in the **Confirm** box to make sure there are no typos
3. Click **Next**

**What makes a strong password?**
- At least 12 characters
- Mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols
- NOT something obvious like "password123" or your birthday

> **Tip:** Write your password down somewhere safe or use a password manager. Google WILL ask for it again, and if you forget it, recovery can be difficult.

## Step 5: Verify Your Phone Number

Google asks for a phone number to prove you're a real person (not a bot) and to help you recover your account if you get locked out.

1. Select your country from the dropdown
2. Enter your phone number
3. Choose whether to get a **Text message** or **Phone call** with the verification code
4. Click **Next**
5. You'll receive a 6-digit code. Enter it in the box and click **Verify**

> **Your phone number is NOT shared publicly.** Google only uses it for security and account recovery.

## Step 6: Complete Your Profile

Fill in the remaining information:

1. **Recovery email** (optional but recommended): Another email address Google can use to reach you if you lose access to this account
2. **Birthday**: Enter your birthdate. Google requires this to verify you're old enough (13+ in most countries)
3. **Gender** (optional): Choose an option or select "Rather not say"
4. Click **Next**
5. Review Google's Privacy and Terms, then click **I agree**

You're done! You'll be taken to your new Gmail inbox.

## Still Stuck?

- **Didn't get the verification code?** Wait 30 seconds and click "Resend." Make sure your phone has signal and isn't blocking unknown numbers.
- **"This phone number has been used too many times"?** Google limits how many accounts can use one phone number. Try a different number, or wait a few days.
- **Want to add this to your phone?** Download the Gmail app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android), then sign in with your new email and password.
