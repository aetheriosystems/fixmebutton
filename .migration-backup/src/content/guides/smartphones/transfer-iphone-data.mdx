## Before You Start

Make sure both iPhones are charged to at least 50%. The transfer can take up to 45 minutes, and you don't want either phone to die halfway through.

**What you'll need:**
- Your old iPhone
- Your new iPhone  
- A stable WiFi connection
- Your Apple ID and password

> **Forgot your Apple ID password?** Tap "Forgot Password" on the sign-in screen before starting.

## Step 1: Turn On Your New iPhone

Press and hold the side button on your new iPhone until the Apple logo appears on the screen. This usually takes about 5-10 seconds.

## Step 2: Follow the Setup Screens

Your new iPhone will show a "Hello" screen in multiple languages. Swipe up from the bottom to begin setup.

- Select your language
- Select your country or region
- Connect to your WiFi network

## Step 3: Look for the Quick Start Screen

After connecting to WiFi, you'll see a screen that says "Quick Start." Bring your **old iPhone** close to your **new iPhone**. A popup will appear on your old iPhone asking if you want to set up your new iPhone.

Tap **Continue** on your old iPhone.

## Step 4: Wait for the Animation

A blue, swirling animation will appear on your new iPhone. Hold your old iPhone over the new one so the animation is centered in the viewfinder on your old iPhone. This pairs the two devices.

## Step 5: Enter Your Old Passcode

Your new iPhone will ask for your old iPhone's passcode. Enter the passcode you used on your old device.

## Step 6: Set Up Face ID or Touch ID

Follow the on-screen instructions to set up Face ID (or Touch ID) on your new iPhone. You can also choose "Set Up Later" if you prefer.

## Step 7: Choose How to Transfer

You'll see three options:
- **Transfer from iPhone** (recommended — fastest)
- **Download from iCloud**
- **Don't Transfer**

Choose **Transfer from iPhone**. Keep both phones close together and plugged into power if possible.

## Step 8: Wait for the Transfer

A progress bar will show on both phones. This can take 15-45 minutes depending on how much data you have. Do not move the phones away from each other during the transfer.

> **Tip:** If the transfer seems stuck, don't panic. Some files (like large video libraries) can take a while with no visible progress.

## Step 9: Complete Apple ID Settings

After the transfer, you may need to enter your Apple ID password on the new iPhone to activate iMessage, FaceTime, and iCloud.

## Step 10: Wait for Apps to Download

Your apps will begin downloading automatically. They'll appear grayed out with a progress circle. This can take 30+ minutes depending on how many apps you have.

## Step 11: Check Your Data

Once everything is done, check that your important data made it over:
- Photos
- Contacts
- Messages
- Notes
- Passwords (Settings → Passwords)

## Step 12: Erase Your Old iPhone (Optional)

Once you're sure everything is on your new iPhone, go to **Settings → General → Transfer or Reset iPhone → Erase All Content and Settings** on your old iPhone.

## Still Stuck?

If the transfer didn't work, try these alternatives:
1. Restart both iPhones and try again
2. Use an iCloud backup instead (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup → Back Up Now, then choose "Download from iCloud" on step 7)
3. Connect both phones with a cable for a wired transfer
